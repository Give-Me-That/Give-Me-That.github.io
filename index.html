Abstract （摘要）

Intuitive human‑robot interaction (HRI，人机交互) is crucial for enabling robots to act as assistive partners...

Method Overview 方法概述

Stage (a): Intent Recognition — Intuitive pointing (手势指示) selects the target object and conveys grasp intent.
Stage (b): Fast Pose Retrieval — Retrieve candidate grasps from a shape‑centric database (形状中心化抓取库).
Stage (c): Grasp Retargeting & Execution — Retarget the retrieved pose to the current scene and execute safely in real time.
  • Shape‑centric grasp database — Covers 4 shape categories (27 instances), built with ~5 min collection + ~8 min processing; easily extensible.
  • Lightweight retrieval framework — Intuitive, safe, and controllable HRI (人机交互) with low latency.
  • Real‑world results — High success on seen/unseen objects; inference latency 0.148 s.
